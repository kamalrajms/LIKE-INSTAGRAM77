Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers.

Is SocialHub free to use?

Yes! SocialHub is completely free to use with all core features included. We also offer premium plans for advanced analytics, extra storage, and priority support.

How secure is my data on SocialHub?

We use end-to-end encryption, two-factor authentication, and comply with GDPR regulations. Your data is never sold to third parties and you have full control over your privacy settings.

Can I live stream in HD quality?

Absolutely! SocialHub supports up to 1080p HD live streaming with real-time chat, reactions, and the ability to invite guests to your stream. It works seamlessly on both mobile and desktop.

What makes the AI Content Creator special?

Our AI analyzes trending topics, your audience preferences, and your content style to generate personalized captions, hashtags, and content ideas that boost engagement.

Is there a limit on group members?

Free accounts can create groups with up to 500 members. Premium users can create unlimited groups with up to 5,000 members each, along with advanced moderation tools.
